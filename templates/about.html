About Garcia Web Designz

Founded January 1st, 2022 by CEO David Garcia

The Spark

The spark and motivation that inspires me and Garcia Web Designz is my family that have their own businesses and successful careers. They set examples for me to do better and to learn from their mistakes as well.

Garcia Web Designz

Garcia Web Designz aims to help businesses get online via custom-built websites. This company also helps people improve their resumes for their potential job interviews by creating a website based on their experiences. Garcia Web Designz not only helps, but also improves your current design and set up of your website or outright creates a new modern design.

Present plans

The current goals and plans of Garcia Web Designz is to grow big enough to establish a persistant client base. This is where I the owner can no longer work alone and expand. I will have to hire new developers to create new and improved websites. This will also help our future plans to become a independent game developer company.

Future goals

In the future when Garcia Web Designz is just big enough to create something more than websites. I will expand this company and create an independent game comapnay. That company will create high quality immersive video games built with integrity and well written stories on console and PC for the people.
